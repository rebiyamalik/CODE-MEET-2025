Event Description

CODE MEET 2025 is a hands on seminar on Agentic AI, led by Rebiya Ismail, designed for students and aspiring developers. Learn Git, GitHub, VS Code, CLI (cline), and GitHub Copilot with practical exercises, collaborative workflows, and real-world project tips. Organized under the guidance of Dr. Imran Javaid and Dr. Athar Kharal at the Center for Advanced Studies in Pure and Applied Mathematics (CASPAM), this seminar bridges theory and practice, empowering participants to build AI-driven projects efficiently. Join us for this exciting and educational event and take your first step toward mastering modern coding tools, AI collaboration, and development workflows
